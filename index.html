REAL PHYSICS

Kobayashi anisotropic phase-field with latent heat, dilute-alloy solute, directional gradients, moving weld pools, and growth twinning — down to the 12-branched snowflake. Verified against the canonical dendrite morphology: not a particle toy. Constitutional undercooling, columnar-to-equiaxed transitions, and recalescence all emerge from the equations, never from scripting. Freeze it as steel, aluminum, magnesium, zinc, or ice — or compose your own alloy element by element and watch its growth restriction factor refine the grains.

TEN LENSES

Incandescent melt, cross-polarised grains, etched micrograph with a scale bar, thermal cam, SEM, X-ray radiograph, growth rings, curvature. Plus a pixel mode for the retro among us.

ENGINEER IT

Live ASTM grain-size metallography, and an onboard CMA-ES optimizer that learns casting schedules to hit a target grain size. Think you can beat it? Challenge mode deals you both the same hand.
