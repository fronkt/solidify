Every cast metal part — engine blocks, turbine blades, the frame of your bike — froze from a melt, and its strength was decided in that moment. SOLIDIFY solves the Kobayashi phase-field equations on your GPU in real time: undercool a melt, tap to nucleate crystals, and watch dendrites grow, branch, collide, and become the grain structure a metallurgist would etch and measure.

REAL PHYSICS

Anisotropic phase-field + latent heat, dilute-alloy solute, directional gradients, moving weld pools. Verified against the canonical dendrite morphology — not a particle toy.

TEN LENSES

Incandescent melt, cross-polarised grains, etched micrograph, thermal cam, SEM, X-ray radiograph, growth rings, curvature, neon contours — one growth, ten instruments.

ENGINEER IT

Live ASTM grain-size metallography, and an onboard CMA-ES optimizer that learns casting schedules to hit a target grain size — race it yourself in challenge mode.

RAW WEBGPU

A million cells stepped thousands of times per second, 60 fps, no frameworks, ~15 kB gzipped. Needs a WebGPU browser (Chrome/Edge, Safari 26+, recent Firefox).
